Notes on GHZ States

Nov 28, 2025

The GHZ state for three qubits is given by:

$$ |\text{GHZ}\rangle = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} (|000\rangle + |111\rangle) $$

Bipartite Reductions

Tracing out one qubit gives a mixed state with non-zero entropy.

The von Neumann entropy is defined as $$ S(\rho) = -\mathrm{Tr}(\rho \log \rho) $$
